NatureCrunch, known for its organic and gluten-free products, has also demonstrated the effectiveness of its marketing campaigns. In 2023, the campaign "Nature's Delight" involved an investment of $480,000, achieving an ROI of 145% and a sales increase of 31%. This campaign reached 2.2 million users on social media, with a success ratio of 46%. Key results include improved engagement with the audience and a significant increase in sales.

In 2022, NatureCrunch launched the campaign "Healthy Munchies" with an investment of $390,000. The ROI was 129%, and sales increased by 27%.
