Report for SnackHeaven

SnackHeaven, an innovative company in the production of healthy snacks, has decided to enhance its profits and expand its market share through an effective marketing campaign. Currently, SnackHeaven's annual revenue is $5,000,000, with a market share of 10%. For this campaign, a budget of $520,000 has been allocated with the goal of significantly increasing market presence and improving profitability.

In 2023, SnackHeaven launched the campaign "Heavenly Bites" with an investment of $520,000. This campaign achieved an ROI of 152%, with a sales increase of 32%. The social media reach was 2.1 million users, with a success ratio of 48%. Key results include a boosted awareness and increased sales for SnackHeaven's products.

In 2022, the campaign "Delightful Flavors" of SnackHeaven had an investment of $300,000 and achieved an ROI of 125%. This campaign resulted in a sales increase of 22% and reached 1.3 million users on social media, with a success ratio of 35%. Despite the lower investment, the results were positive, strengthening the brand's image as committed to health.
