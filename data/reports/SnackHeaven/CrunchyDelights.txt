Report for CrunchyDelights

CrunchyDelights has implemented various successful marketing campaigns in recent years. In 2023, the campaign "Crunch Time" involved an investment of $600,000 and achieved a return on investment (ROI) of 160%. This campaign resulted in a sales increase of 35% and reached 2.5 million users on social media, with a success ratio of 50%. Key results of this campaign include a significant increase in brand visibility on social media and a notable improvement in sales.

In 2022, CrunchyDelights launched the campaign "Deliciously Crunchy" with an investment of $270,000. This campaign achieved an ROI of 122%, with a sales increase of 20%. The social media reach was 1.2 million users, and the success ratio was 33%. Despite the lower investment compared to the 2023 campaign, the results were impressive, building the online presence of CrunchyDelights.
